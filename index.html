About Me

Hello, I am Eluned. I have travelled to Walt Disney World many times over the last 15 years. I have only stayed at either Disney Hotels or partmer hotels that are located on Disney property. Disney has so much to offer alongside the rides and roller coasters with special events happening throughout the year. This website details some of the things I have done, where I have stayed, the restaurants I have eaten in and activites that you would not associate with Disney.

Transport Options

There are many modes of transport to get around Disney. Disney Buses operate between the Parks, the resorts and Disney Springs. There are Water Boats that operate between Hollywood Studios and Epcot stopping at The Boardwalk and Yacht and Beach Club resorts. You can ride high in the sky on the Disney Monorail with routes from Epcot to the Magic Kingdom. The latest addition is the Disney Skyliner This connects Disney's Pop Century, Riviera Resort, Caribbean Beach resort and The Art of Animation Resort to the magic with stations at Epcot and Hollywood Studios.

Disney Springs

Disney Springs is a large shopping and dining complex. Disney Springs has Disney themed stores, but also named brands such as Pandora, Superdry etc. There are a large range of dining options includinh Wolfgang Puck and House of Blues.
